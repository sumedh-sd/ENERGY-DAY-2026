Past Competitions

Energenius Hackathon

Machine Learning for Energy Systems

The Energenius Hackathon challenges participants to develop machine learning-powered platforms addressing critical energy management applications. Teams design and prototype intelligent systems capable of fuel consumption prediction, anomaly detection in energy infrastructure, and operational optimization through advanced data analytics and visualization dashboards. The competition emphasizes practical implementation of artificial intelligence and machine learning methodologies in industrial energy contexts.

Energy Case Challenge

Strategic Analysis & Business Solutions

A national-level case competition that challenges teams to analyze complex energy sector challenges and develop comprehensive strategic solutions. Participants engage with real-world business problems in renewable energy integration, grid optimization, energy policy development, and sustainable infrastructure deployment. The competition tests analytical capabilities, strategic thinking, and the ability to translate technical knowledge into actionable business recommendations.

Watt's the Answer!

Energy-Themed Quiz Competition

An engaging energy-themed quiz competition that tests participants' knowledge across various energy topics, from fundamental concepts to current industry developments. The competition features multiple rounds including rapid-fire questions, visual rounds, and buzzer rounds, covering renewable energy technologies, energy policy, sustainable development, and emerging trends in the energy sector. Designed to encourage knowledge sharing and healthy competition among energy enthusiasts.
