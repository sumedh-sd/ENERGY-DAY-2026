About Energy Day

Energy Day is the biggest annual event for our department here at IIT Bombay. It's the one time of year we bring everyone together - students, professors, and industry experts - to move beyond textbooks and actually talk about the future of energy.

Over two days, students, academics, industry professionals, and researchers converge to explore cutting-edge technologies, participate in competitions, and network with leading figures in the energy sector.

  • Networking Sessions
