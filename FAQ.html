Have Questions? We Have Answers

What is Energy Day?
+
Energy Day is a flagship annual platform hosted by the Department of Energy Science and Engineering at IIT Bombay. It brings together industry leaders, researchers, policymakers, academics, and students to showcase research capabilities, explore emerging energy technologies, and foster collaboration across the energy ecosystem.
What are the registration requirements?
+
Registration policies vary by participant category. While registration is typically free for students and academic attendees, industry and corporate participants may be subject to separate registration guidelines.
When and where will Energy Day 2026 be held?
+
Energy Day 2026 will be held on April 11th, 2026, at the IIT Bombay Research Park, Powai, Mumbai. The event will feature technical sessions, research showcases, and facility visits.
Who is eligible to attend?
+
Energy Day is open to industry professionals, corporate representatives, researchers, faculty members, students, and policymakers with an interest in the energy sector. The event is designed to facilitate cross-sector engagement and knowledge exchange.
What competitions are available?
+
Energy Day includes a range of technical and analytical competitions such as hackathons, case challenges, and quizzes. These activities are designed to encourage innovation and problem-solving across academic and industry participants.
What accommodation options are available?
+
Limited on-campus accommodation at IIT Bombay may be available, subject to availability and applicable guidelines. Participants requiring accommodation are advised to contact energyday@iitb.ac.in for further information.
What networking opportunities are provided?
+
Energy Day offers structured networking sessions, industry interactions, and a dedicated career and collaboration forum. These platforms enable participants to engage with corporate representatives, explore partnerships, and discuss research, internships, and professional opportunities.
How can I present my research?
+
Energy Day features dedicated research presentation and poster sessions that provide participants with an opportunity to showcase ongoing work, technological developments, and applied research initiatives. Interested participants should contact energyday@iitb.ac.in for submission guidelines.
