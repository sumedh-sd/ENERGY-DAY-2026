A Closer Look at Energy Day 2025

AI, Capital, and Clean Energy: Trifecta Powering a Sustainable Future

Mr. Vineet Mittal delivered a keynote examining the strategic convergence of artificial intelligence, capital investment, and clean energy technologies. The session explored how this triad is reshaping global energy systems and accelerating sustainable infrastructure development.

Mr. Vineet Mittal
Chairman, Avaada Group

The Renewable Energy Paradox: Are We Ignoring The Environmental Cost?

Leading experts critically examined often-overlooked environmental implications of renewable energy infrastructure. The panel analyzed resource extraction, manufacturing processes, and lifecycle impacts, fostering nuanced perspectives on balancing sustainability objectives with comprehensive environmental assessments.

Energy Case Challenge

The Energy Case Challenge 2025, powered by INA Solar, concluded with finalist presentations from over 700 national registrations. Teams presented comprehensive strategies addressing renewable energy integration, grid optimization, and sustainable infrastructure deployment, demonstrating advanced analytical capabilities and industry-relevant problem-solving approaches.

Energenius Hackathon

The second edition of Energenius Hackathon, powered by Repos Energy, challenged participants to develop ML-powered platforms for fuel consumption prediction, anomaly detection, and operational optimization. Teams produced functional prototypes with applications in industrial energy management and efficiency enhancement.

Lab Visits & Research Exhibition

Participants accessed state-of-the-art research facilities at IIT Bombay's Department of Energy Science and Engineering. The laboratory tours showcased experimental setups and ongoing research in power systems, energy storage, thermal management, and renewable energy applications, enabling direct interaction between industry professionals and research faculty.

Student Research Presentations

Students presented cutting-edge research across power systems and grid integration, energy storage systems, renewable and sustainable energy technologies, and thermal management in industrial processes. The presentations demonstrated innovative approaches to contemporary energy challenges and research excellence.
